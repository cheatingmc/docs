## Manual purchasing via Discord

### Getting started with the Discord bot

<Steps>
<Step title="Join our Discord server">
  Head over to our Discord community to access the bot and purchase accounts manually.
  
  <Card
    title="Join Discord"
    icon="discord"
    href="https://discord.gg/nicealts"
  >
    Click here to join our Discord server
  </Card>
</Step>

<Step title="Purchase credits">
  Use the bot commands to buy credits for your account:
  
  ```
  /buy-credits
  ```
  
  Credits are used to purchase Minecraft accounts and can be bought with various payment methods.
</Step>

<Step title="Browse available accounts">
  Check what accounts are currently available:
  
  ```
  /stock
  ```
  
  This shows you all available account types, prices, and current stock levels.
</Step>

<Step title="Purchase accounts">
  Buy accounts using your credits:
  
  ```
  /buy [account-type] [quantity]
  ```
  
  Example: `/buy unban 2` to purchase 2 unbanned accounts.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Account types explained

### AccessToken accounts
- **Format:** `email:password:access_token`
- **Use case:** Most applications and tools
- **Quality:** High reliability and fast delivery
- **Prechecking:** Available to validate accounts before delivery

### Cookie accounts
- **Format:** `email:password:cookie_data`
- **Use case:** Advanced applications requiring session data
- **Quality:** Premium quality with session information
- **Prechecking:** Available for quality assurance

<Info>
**Prechecking:** Optional validation that ensures accounts are working and not banned before delivery. Adds a small delay but guarantees quality.
</Info>

## Payment and credits

### How credits work
- Credits are your account balance for purchasing accounts
- 1 credit = 1 unit of account value
- Credits are tied to your Discord account
- Purchase credits using various payment methods through the Discord bot

### Payment methods
- **Cryptocurrency** - Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major cryptocurrencies
- **PayPal** - Direct PayPal payments
- **Credit/Debit cards** - Traditional payment methods
- **Gift cards** - Various gaming and retail gift cards

### Credit pricing
- Credits are sold in packages with volume discounts
- Check current pricing in Discord using `/credits-pricing`
- Bulk purchases offer better rates
