## Step-by-step purchase process

<Steps>
<Step title="Choose a payment method">
  Click on your preferred payment method above to be redirected to the shop:
  - **Cryptocurrency** - For BTC and LTC payments
  - **PayPal** - For PayPal payments
  - **Credit/Debit Cards** - For card payments
</Step>

<Step title="Buy credits">
  Complete your purchase on the shop website using your chosen payment method.
</Step>

<Step title="Redeem your credits">
  Once you receive your credit code, use this command in Discord:
  
  ```
  /redeem [your-code]
  ```
  
  Replace `[your-code]` with the actual code you received from your purchase.
</Step>

<Step title="Credits added">
  Your credits will be automatically added to your account balance. You can check your balance using `/balance`.
</Step>
</Steps>


## Checking your balance

### View current balance

Use this command to check your current credit balance:

```
/balance
```

This shows:
- Current credit balance
- Recent transactions
- Account status
- Available for purchases

### Transaction history

View your credit purchase history:

```
/history
```

This displays:
- Recent credit purchases
- Payment methods used
- Transaction amounts
- Purchase dates

## Troubleshooting

### Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Redeem code not working">
  **Problem:** `/redeem` command not accepting your code
  **Solutions:**
  - Double-check the code for typos
  - Make sure the code hasn't been used already
  - Wait a few minutes after purchase before redeeming
  - Contact support with your purchase details
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Credits not added after redeem">
  **Problem:** Redeem successful but balance not updated
  **Solutions:**
  - Use `/balance` to check your current balance
  - Wait a few minutes for the system to update
  - Try the redeem command again
  - Contact support if the issue persists
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Purchase issues on shop">
  **Problem:** Having trouble with the shop website
  **Solutions:**
  - Check your internet connection
  - Try a different browser
  - Clear your browser cache
  - Contact the shop support directly
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for successful purchases

### Before purchasing

- **Check current pricing** - Use `/credits-pricing` for latest rates
- **Choose right amount** - Consider your usage needs
- **Have payment method ready** - Ensure your payment method is available

### During purchase

- **Follow shop instructions** - Complete the purchase process on the shop website
- **Save your code** - Keep the credit code you receive after purchase
- **Don't lose the code** - You'll need it to redeem credits

### After purchase

- **Redeem quickly** - Use `/redeem [code]` as soon as you get your code
- **Check your balance** - Use `/balance` to verify credits were added
- **Test with small purchase** - Try buying a cheap account first
- **Contact support if needed** - We're here to help
