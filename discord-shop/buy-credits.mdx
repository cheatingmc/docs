## Overview

Credits are your account balance for purchasing Minecraft accounts. This guide shows you how to buy credits through our Discord bot using various payment methods.

## Getting started

### Step 1: Join our Discord server

First, you'll need to join our Discord community:

<Card
  title="Join Discord"
  icon="discord"
  href="https://discord.gg/nicealts"
>
  Click here to join our Discord server
</Card>

### Step 2: Access the credit purchase system

Once you're in the server, use the credit purchase command:

```
/buy-credits
```

This will open the credit purchase interface where you can select your preferred payment method and amount.

## Step-by-step purchase process

### Using the Discord bot

<Steps>
<Step title="Start the purchase process">
  Use the command in Discord:
  
  ```
  /buy-credits
  ```
  
  The bot will show you available payment methods and credit packages.
</Step>

<Step title="Select payment method">
  Choose your preferred payment method from the available options:
  - Cryptocurrency
  - PayPal
  - Credit/Debit Card
  - Gift Card
</Step>

<Step title="Choose credit amount">
  Select the credit package that fits your needs:
  - Starter (100 credits)
  - Regular (500 credits)
  - Pro (1000 credits)
  - Custom amount (if available)
</Step>

<Step title="Complete payment">
  Follow the payment instructions provided by the bot:
  - **Crypto:** Send to the provided wallet address
  - **PayPal:** Complete the PayPal checkout
  - **Card:** Enter your card details securely
  - **Gift Card:** Provide the gift card code
</Step>

<Step title="Receive credits">
  Once payment is confirmed, credits will be automatically added to your account. You'll receive a confirmation message.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Payment method details

### Cryptocurrency payments

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How to pay with crypto">
  1. Select "Cryptocurrency" as your payment method
  2. Choose your preferred coin (BTC, ETH, LTC, etc.)
  3. Send the exact amount to the provided wallet address
  4. Wait for blockchain confirmation (usually 10-30 minutes)
  5. Credits will be added automatically once confirmed
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Supported cryptocurrencies">
  - **Bitcoin (BTC)** - Most popular, widely accepted
  - **Ethereum (ETH)** - Fast transactions, lower fees
  - **Litecoin (LTC)** - Fast and cheap transactions
  - **Bitcoin Cash (BCH)** - Low fees, fast processing
  - **USDT (Tether)** - Stable value, widely accepted
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Crypto payment tips">
  - Always send the exact amount shown
  - Double-check the wallet address before sending
  - Keep your transaction ID for support if needed
  - Don't send from exchanges that require manual approval
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

### Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Payment not processing">
  **Problem:** Payment completed but credits not added
  **Solutions:**
  - Wait 5-10 minutes for processing
  - Check transaction status in Discord
  - Contact support with transaction details
  - Verify payment was sent to correct address
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Insufficient funds">
  **Problem:** Payment method declined due to insufficient funds
  **Solutions:**
  - Check account balance
  - Try a different payment method
  - Purchase a smaller credit package
  - Contact your bank if using a card
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Crypto payment delayed">
  **Problem:** Cryptocurrency payment taking too long
  **Solutions:**
  - Check blockchain confirmation status
  - Higher network fees = faster processing
  - Contact support with transaction hash
  - Wait for network congestion to clear
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Gift card rejected">
  **Problem:** Gift card code not accepted
  **Solutions:**
  - Verify code is correct and unused
  - Check gift card is from supported retailer
  - Ensure gift card has sufficient value
  - Contact support for manual verification
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for successful purchases

### Before purchasing

- **Check current pricing** - Use `/credits-pricing` for latest rates
- **Verify payment method** - Ensure your payment method is ready
- **Choose right amount** - Consider your usage needs
- **Check network status** - For crypto, check network congestion

### During purchase

- **Follow instructions exactly** - Don't skip any steps
- **Double-check amounts** - Especially for crypto payments
- **Keep transaction details** - Save confirmation numbers
- **Be patient** - Some methods take time to process

### After purchase

- **Verify credits added** - Check your balance with `/balance`
- **Save transaction info** - Keep for your records
- **Test with small purchase** - Try buying a cheap account first
- **Contact support if needed** - We're here to help
